# TON Premium Access — Subscriber FAQ

## How do I subscribe?
- Open the payment link from the admin → connect TON wallet → pay the exact amount shown → wait 1–2 minutes for confirmation → you’ll receive an invite link in this chat.

## I paid but didn’t get an invite link
- Wait 2 minutes, then open the bot chat again.
- Make sure you paid the exact amount and confirmed the transaction in your wallet.

## My invite link says expired / invalid
- Invite links are single‑use and time‑limited for security.
- Contact the group admin to get a new one (or retry from the payment link if your subscription isn’t active).

## Can I renew early?
- Yes. Renewing before expiry extends your subscription (remaining days + new days).

## Refunds
- Crypto payments are final; refunds are not automated. Contact the admin if you need help.
