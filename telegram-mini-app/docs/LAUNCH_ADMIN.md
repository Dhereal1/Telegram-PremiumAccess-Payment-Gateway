# TON Premium Access — Admin Launch Guide

Welcome to TON Premium Access.

## What you get
- Subscribers pay in TON and get access automatically.
- Expiry is enforced automatically (members removed when subscription ends).
- Track earnings + manage groups in the Admin Dashboard.

## Setup (3 steps)
1) Add `@tonpremium_access_bot` to your private group as an **admin** (must have permission to invite users).
2) DM the bot and complete the group setup (price in TON, duration in days, group name).
3) Open **Admin Dashboard** → connect your TON wallet (TonConnect) → tap **Save Wallet** (required to receive payments).

## How subscribers join
- You share your bot payment link (from the dashboard).
- Subscribers click it → pay in TON → they receive a **single‑use invite link** to join.

## Rules / limitations (important)
- Group must be **private**.
- Bot must stay **admin**; if you remove it, new subscribers can’t be added.
- TON only (USDT coming later).
- Recommended minimum price: **0.1 TON** (reduces spam/abuse).

## Support / troubleshooting
- If a subscriber paid but didn’t receive the invite link: ask them to open the bot chat again (they may have muted/blocked DMs).
